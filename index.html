About the Shed

Minimal bureaucracy.
Maximum fun.

The Yass Valley Men's Shed is a welcoming community space where men of all ages and backgrounds can come together. Whether you want to work on a project, pick up a new skill, or just enjoy good company over a coffee, there is a place for you here.

We have a well-equipped workshop with woodworking and metalworking tools. Our members are happy to help, share what they know, or just have a yarn.

What we offer

Something for everyone

Whatever brings you through the door, you will find something useful at the Shed.

Workshop Access

Use our woodworking and metalworking workshop during opening hours. Work on your own projects or help out with group ones.

Community & Connection

Meet men from around Yass Valley. Have a chat, share stories, and make new mates. The social side matters just as much as the work.

Learn New Skills

Our members are happy to pass on what they know. It does not matter if you are an experienced tradie or picking up a tool for the first time.

Monthly Highlight

No tools, just talk.

On the first Wednesday of every month, we put down the tools and focus on conversation. The workshops are quiet so everyone can hear each other.

Woolworths Yass kindly donates cakes and pastries. We sometimes have short talks on topics members find interesting, or other entertainment. It is a popular session.
